[[LANGUAGE]] Nouns and Articles

In these paragraphs explain anything you need to know about nouns and indefinite articles for your language. (The definite article is covered in a later lesson.) This includes whether nouns and articles have genders, and how many genders or classes there are, and what they are called. This format is really mean for languages with zero to three genders. For languages with a large number of noun classes you might only want to introduce two or three of them at this point.

If there is no indefinite article in your language, then mention that. Give examples of a couple nouns with indefinite articles, if any:

[[A BOOK]]
a book
[[A PEN]]
a pen

Here explain whether verbs inflect for person and number. At this point you only need first person and second person singular. Give the two examples below.

[[I HAVE]]
I have
[[YOU HAVE]]
you have
