General webwork

These are works that ultimately belong in a page of its own and not in any other website.

Ponies on Lyr: Letters from an Observer

Ponies on Lyr: Letters from an Observer (also known by its acronym POLLFAO, which is pronounced [polfɐ]) is a fanfic that began approximately Mid-June 2011. It's partially a xenoshock fic that is set within another, previously unrelated place known as Lyr, and is also an experiment in using full HTML(5) to tell a story that is textual in nature – filled with title texts, sidebars and footnotes, and of course the ubiquitous <-- HTML comments -->, it promotes a non-linear reading order.

It partially needs JavaScript to fully embellish certain places, and on occasion, the speaker is also used.

Status Altering Subtances

Status Altering Substances (abbreviated as SAS) is a more traditional and conservative pony fiction (yes I am aware that a lot of this kind of thing is made but it's fun to make and that's all that matters).

Anyway, this particular pony fiction, co-worked with someone else, talks about the adventures of the, ahem, "Manesix" (pardon the air/scare quotes) inerbriated under a certain amount of chemical that we are proud to call "sopor". Yup, that one.

The Beginner's Guide to Alternia

The (or A, as it sometimes is) Beginner's Guide to Alternia is a very unconventional Homestuck fanfic. (Yes, I can do that!) Rather than being a generic Sburb clone, it mainly deals with the planet of Alternia (which is pretty obvious given the title) and the merits of the planet itself, rather than any of its Trolls and other sapiogenerated things.

Utilitettes

Utilitettes is a catch-all word for webpages that offer to do one thing and one thing only, often something limited in scope or usage. Due to the interactive nature of these webpages JavaScript is a must.

MSPA Online Random Ship Generator

The MSPA-ORSG is a random ship generator for the forumites that participate in forumite shipping. It basically generates them with very little of what to do with them. The first and foremost webwork I have done, and is the one that kick-started everything else.

LNIIS:HS/FT Generator

In my ongoing quest to make every character in the MSPA universe indexable, I have stumbled upon the fact that, under a technicality, all "fantrolls" – characters with gray skin and bright orange horns that are not made by a certain Mr. Hussie – are "canon", meaning that they are included in this scheme. I have developed this little thing to keep track of all the trolls that have done.

The future of this particular utilitette is probably a database system for all the fantrolls. Find it here.
