Note to headers

All values except planet counts are in millions, and are accurate to the nearest 0.1 million.

Monolingual L1
Speakers who exclusively speak Alternian, and furthermore either never had any knowledge of other langauges, or once learnt other languages but have since forgotten them.
Monolingual L2 + L1
Speakers who exclusively speak Alternian, but retains some working knowledge of the underlying language they once spoke.
Monolingual L2 − L1
Speakers who exclusively speak Alternian, and once spoke the underlying langauge but had since lost any working components of that language.
Multilingual L1
Speakers who speak Alternian as a primary language, and also know and use a second langauge (frequently the underlying language).
Multilingual L2
Speakers who speak another language, and have acquired Alternian in a later stage.
Multilingual L3+
Speakers who speak another language, acquires one or more other languages, and finally acquires Alternian.
