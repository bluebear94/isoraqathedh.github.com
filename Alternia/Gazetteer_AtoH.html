A

Aew-xrriad-nij Desert

Coordinates: 155°W, 24°S F

A great desert, one of the driest known in Quuag-dulle. While not as big as the Great Ecchiae Desert, the desert is far drier and has no major rivers flowing down it. It encompasses much of Hrheew-yrrvu.

Akinaks Sea

Coordinates: 0°, 0° S

A large and shallow part of the larger Idjae Ocean, often averaging no more than 150 metres down. Prime coral reef territory! This sea also covers the coveted origin of the coordinate system to which every point on Alternia finds reference to – and what else to put there than the Grand Palace of the Alternian Empire?

B

Bakijivu

Coordinates: 0°, 45°N F

The northwest portion of the continent of Ecchiae, Bakijivu is a big flat plain that has long been viewed as the food heartland of Alternia.

Bivu

Coordinates: 90°W, 0° F

A giant peninsula curving off of Sewb! It first heads southeast, then south, then it ends in a large volcanic arc, named the Ieakka islands.

Bivusti Mountains

Coordinates: 89°W, 0°S Magnos Bastil Bivu

The Bivusti mountains are an ancient fold montain range that lives in Bivu. The mountains are fairly weathered and usually are not very tall, and don't support a major river system, but are taller and younger than the sister Maknos mountains in Oae, further south, parellels the Bivusti but are otherwise unrelated.

Bthebb

Coordinates: 87°W, 18°S F

The largest islands of the Iekka islands, Bthebb is a large island with a great big volcano to boot. The volcano is active though, and sometimes it can blow off its top – most recently in −15 PD.

C

Cchissin

Coordinates: 156°W, 62°N Cizin Nakolep

A small island. It's hard to land here, due to virulent growths that plague all but sheltered coves and caves, so mainly it's just a navigational hazard. It is, however, a biological haven, with an intense microecosystem utterly different to anything on Earth or Alternia.

D

Deiokup

Coordinates: 81°W, 21°S F

An island of the Iekkas, and the third largest by area. It generally acts as a liasion with the smaller islands to the south and east. Beyond this point, the sea usaully keeps continually shallow but high in relief, causing large fishing grounds and coral reefs!

Djekth

Coordinates: 90°W, 60°N F

Djekth is an eternal Nordic wonderland! While there aren't as many mountains, the forests here are fairly abundant, sometimes even mirroring the thick supergrowth islands that the distant islands of Cchissin and Nakkohlep have.

Djoffa Ocean

Coordinates: 120°E, 15°S Joviaa (Luppit), potentially Jaffa cakes

The smallest ocean in Alternia; it encompasses most of the water south of the equator and between 80°E to 130°W. Unlike the other two oceans, there's no strong spreading ridge under the Djoffa.

Dwesdjae River

Coordinates: 30°E, 0° Jesjae Oltaui

A river that starts off at the Central Preenche Plateau and goes down to Otavuw bay.

Dwie River

Coordinates: 46°E, 30°N Teddie Specer

A long river that eventually drains into Xrij. The whole river is a vital lifeline to the forests that surround Xrij, and is also responsible for fertilizing the east of said forest. Other short rivers feed the east part.

E

Ea

Coordinates: 30°W, 28°S S

The northernmost continent in Alternia! It sure won't feel like it though – the warm climate ensures taht this place will never leave you with an icy reception. Cultural-wise, the whole continent is usually a factory-cum-Training Grounds place, and also a gulag. There are plenty of pretty scenes to look at though!

Ecchiae

Coordinates: 0°, 30°N "Asia"

The largest continent in Alternia. A fat, squat continent, it's generally split – geographically and culturally – into Oae and Bakijivu on the North and West, Ssiounsdu on the East, and Khrrup on the South, along with the Great Ecchiae Desert in the centre.

Ecchiae, Great Desert

Coordinates: 0°, 30°N "Asia"

An extensive desert that covers a large portion of central Ecchiyae. It's the result of a rainshadow of the Imelle mountain range, along with just plain old altitude and length of the field!

Edda Forest

Coordinates: 90°E, 60°S F

A large and pristine forest, watered by frontal rain and long summer days. The dampness of the place does sometimes mean that you can find supergrowth in this biome! They probably were blown over from nearby islands and found good living space here.

Eegsou Forest

Coordinates: 15°W, 0°S S

Another large and dense redwood forest that spans most of the Kvumsiu River. Due to the river also supplying more water, this place is very thick in forests and there is abundant wildlife here.

F

G

Gfia

Coordinates: 86°W, 21°S F

An island of the Iekkas, and the second largest by area. It also hosts a whole city in and of itself, a vast network of ferries keep it connected.

Giae

Coordinates: 86°W, 21°S F

An island of the Iekkas. It allows connection between Bthebb and Gfia by means of a waystation.

H

Hrheew-yrrvu

Coordinates: 150°W, 0° F

This hard-to-pronounce place is fairly dry, because of the influence of the Weelas and Nekhttae mountains to the west, in Queewkkae. Nevertheless, it's still a well-lived place, and it's also the hub of many a space launchpad.

Hscanthmou Is.

Coordinates: 102°W, 19°S F

One island in the long and impressive chain of islands that surround the Peetegge Spreading Ridge. Geologically undifferentiated from nearby islands (they're all volcanoes in the tropics!) this island in particular stands out because it is an important waystation for ferries and even some short-range flyers to Quuag-dulle.
