Q

Queewkkae

Coordinates: 180°, 15°S F

The west portion of the continent of Quuag-dulle, Queewkkae is almost the furthest east anyone can go. It's fairly dry, especially its southern locales, due to the precense of a mountain chain – the Nekhttae Mountains – on the furthest west.

Quuag-dulle

Coordinates: 180°, 0° F

This continent is Australia #2 of Alternia. Its relative isolation from the other three continents means that it harbours strange lifeforms unknown to the Mainland. Unfortunately the gap between it and Sewb is a little bit on the short side, meaning that it isn't as wet as Sewb. The foreignness is also reflected in the names – Quuag-dulle names are significantly longer in both character and syllable count.

R

Like J, the design of the Alternian orthography has made it so that toponyms never start with an R in Alternia, as Rs only appear after consonants cannot be complete syllable onsets themselves. Unlike J and Z however, Rs can appear to head words if it succeeds a weak enough consonant. What constitutes a "weak enough consonant" however is not relevant to the discussion here. This section shall stay to ensure the completeness of the alphabet.

S

Saesson Gap

Coordinates: 74°W, 7°N *"(Trol) Seasson"

The Saesson Gap is a small strait that connects the Saesson Sea with the Peetegge Ocean. Because most of the time water is flowing into the Sea rather than out of it, the Gap is saltwater.

Saesson Sea

Coordinates: 60°W, 0° *"(Trol) Seasson"

A large inland sea framed by Ecchiae and Sewb, Saesson is a large inland sea that proves to be a ecological hotspot, as well as a prolific trading location.

Sakin

Coordinates: 93°W, 15°S Aerius Sakino

A wonderful port city that acts as a terminus for most of the ferries and airports that go to Quuag-dulle. The large city is known for its sprawl, height of its buildings (quite tall), and of course a huge grass plain to its north.

Sewb

Coordinates: 60°W, 15°S F

A continent that's currently trying (and failing) to subduct under Ecchiae. The contient is generally linear and is lined with forest, though the inland portions tend to thin out into savanna.

Siggappo

Coordinates: 78°E, 0°N Singapore

The premiere port city of all of Alternia. Living right at the edge of of the Krrops Peninsula, it too has great marshes and redwood forests behind it, like its sister city Tsakkta.

Ssiounsdu

Coordinates: 90°E, 30°N F

The east portion of the continent of Ecchiae, Ssiounsdu is a relatively flat land made of lava spewed from the Imelle mountain range in the distant past, that also defines its western border.

Szrraewp

Coordinates: 44°E, 51°N F

This city lives in between Ecchiae and Ea, and is a great link to the two – at least, one that doesn't pass through miserable bogs.

T

Tuka Sea

Coordinates: 40°W, 20°N Turkey

A deep isolated node of the Saesson Sea, the Tuka serves Wriu, which lives in the innermost (easternmost) portion of it. This sea in particular is shallower than the rest of the Saesson, meaning that in colder times when ice forms in the poles, it can actually become land!

Tsakkta

Coordinates: 59°E, 8°S Jakarta

Another ferry city and port of call to Yiattew. Behind though, lives some of the greatest marshlands in Alternia and beyond, full of life and perhaps even exceeding the Everglades at their peak.

W

Weelas Mountains

Coordinates: 130°W, 20°S Nektan Whelan Wales

A short – both in height and in length – mountain range that came from years long past and has weathered quite severely. Not as severely as the Appalachians, however! This dwarf mountain range still snags whatever little rain and wind that is left that the Nekhttaes can't catch – leaving leeward lands drier than ever! The montains themselves are okay though – endless fog and drizzle from weak winds from the south, making the whole thing feel a bit Welsh.

Wriu

Coordinates: 33°W, 14°N F

Wriu is a large city of 10 million, and is a major port that allows transporation from sea to land. While this place is not particularly geographically interesting, a lot of tours include this city for ease of travel.

Wrro Savanna

Coordinates: 50°W, 15°S S

A vast savanna going on forest to the north and east, Wrro is a large grass plain. There's much speculation that the Trolls started out here. The savanna stands on top of a plateau of the same name, which causes the Savanna to become dry and not as rainy as land of a similar latitude should be.

Wugleth

Coordinates: 30°W, 29°S Wuhan Leithe (Arbied)

A great river city in Ssiounsdu. The city sprawls a lot bigger, and there is a much lower density here.

X

Xrij

Coordinates: 60°W, 30°S F

A verdant forest with arboreal life to match, this forest also gives its name to the land and also a large city that lives in the middle of it all.

Xrij (city)

Coordinates: 30°W, 29°S F

A great city of about 9 million, this is the base of operations of most entertainment things, including the HQ of the Café and of course various game centres have their servers hosted here.

Y

Yiattew

Coordinates: 60°E, 45°S F

The smaller of the two islands Yiattew–Phriutch, Yiattew has long since been the seen as a "best possible place" to live in. Geologically, it's a wonder: mountains and valleys, with crazy fractal-like shorelines to the north.

Yiattew–Phriutch

Coordinates: 90°E, 60°S F

A continent cleft into two by the Peetegge Spreading Ridge, this continent is most isolated and bizarre. A majority of Training Grounds are on this continent, as are some of the most spectacular geology.

Yiwgg (city)

Coordinates: 85°W, 6°S S

Another port of about five million. This city is well known for also residing in places where 100-metre cliffs of limestone are found! Many a Troll has descended – at all speeds – down these.

Yiwgg, Cape

Coordinates: 80°W, 7°S S

A smallish cape that almost cuts off the Yiwgg Sea from the rest of the Idjae Ocean. In cooler times, the cape can creep all the way across the sea to Bivu, cutting off the sea to all else.

Yiwgg Sea

Coordinates: 82°W, 0° S

A small inland sea that is surrounded by Bivu on the west and a cape of the same name on the east. The sea is surprisingly deep for its size and there are strong vents there as well, propelling abyssal life.

Yutkkiop

Coordinates: 83°W, 25°S F

An island of the Iekkas. A verdant reef surrounds it, though the island itself is a far cry from its larger relatives up to the northwest, like Deiokup. The island is usually uninhabited, but there's a temporary shelter station that usually has a tender some months of the long, long year.

Z

Like J, the design of the Alternian orthography has made it so that toponyms never start with a Z in Alternia, as Zs only appear in the multi-character letter "sz" (the Oddball form of "ss"). This section shall stay to ensure the completeness of the alphabet.
