This is a generator of the Loose Letter-Number Interlaced Identification System (a.k.a. LNIIS) of fantrolls in the Homestuck context. It is far more restricted than the one used in the Homestuck Fantrolls context, and as such will require more space. For more information on the LNIIS please see this Wiki article.

Definitions

Horn features

Junctions
Places where three horn segments meet. They add 1 point.
Spikes
Pointy bits on the horn's end. They add 0 points for the first two and 1 point for each pair thereafter. No points are added if there is one left over.
Blobs
Large thicker bits of the horn. They add 2 points.
Bends
Where the horns change direction suddenly. They add 2 points.
Snaps
Parts where the horn has broken off. They add 3 points.
Curves
Parts where the horn noticeably bends. They add 3 points for a pair; If there is one left over after pairing, add 1 point.
