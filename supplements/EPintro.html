Introduction to Egonyota Pasaru

Egonyota Pasaru (EP) is the primary langauge of communication in the Pseudoan Empire. It is the one that (usually) gets top billing in language lists, and it is one of the four languages that must be chosen in an education schema. It also is the first on the list of the Four Standard Languages, along with Serakafph Xaxex (SX), Cipogrtesaj (Ct) and yukũa|Elaga ütæk|Qvalsa (EQ).

EP was born as Egonyota Vohalyosun (E. Vhs), a relatively average eastern bulillan language family; the family is famous for being agglutinative weakly, and also the extreme rarity of possessing a maje-, meso- and miniscule script. It transformed into Egonyota Pasaru – the representative language of the planet – through smallish reforms to some of its more arcane aspect (e.g. bracket subordinates). A consensus was reached 71 545 PDN, after a 100-year-long pocess of reforms, revisions and rehashes.

EP has a very simple VSO PMT system along with #NA and a flexible verb-adverb relationship. The language borrows heavily and lends even more, which is not surprising given its status.

EP has a governing authority but it does not regulate the language – the society is very descriptive and issues a yearly report on changes on the language (yearly + relativity-breaking-magic, of course…) The authority does make annual standardised tests of all sorts around the year, but in limited supply since they aren't required to indicate procifiency of a language (and no one cares about them save a tiny niche anyway.)
